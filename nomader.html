PROJECT OVERVIEW

PROJECT SCOPE

Designing a landing page promoting traveling app for Budget Explorer Nomads

FOCUS

Experimenting with AI in design • UX/UI design • Prototyping • Content creation • Brand identity

TOOLS

Figma • Fig Jam • Adobe Photoshop • Adobe Illustrator • Adobe Firefly • OpenAI

TEAM

4 ppl, Self directed

DURATION

1 Week, SPRINT

CHALLENGE

Familiarizing ourselves with the ways AI can enhance our work as designers, by proposing the challenge of creating a landing page for an imaginary target audience, generated by AI - Budget Explorer Nomads

From ideation to synthesis of data to presentation - we have to utilize the AI tools available in order to learn how to design more effectively for the modern world

SOLUTION

Designing a landing page promoting a traveling app for Budget Explorer Nomads, by integrating AI into all steps of the design process

MY CONTRIBUTION

Ux/ui design

Conceptualizing and gathering relevant data from investigating our target group and how we can create value for them. Wireframing, content creation and creating the app mockups we use in the website

visuals

I experimented with prompts based on our target group and aesthetic for the imagery and graphic elements. Worked on the design and transitioning between sections and created illustrations

execution

Since the aim was to expand our knowledge into the depts of AI, this project only consisted ideating and designing and the final Figma prototype was our end goal

RESEARCH CONCEPTUALIZE DESIGN REFLECTION

research

Our Aim

• Get further information about the AI generated target audience

• Understand their struggles

• Gather potential suggestions for the app we are going to advertise on the landing page

The tools:

• ChatGPT

Findings - The target group

In brief

Adventure-seeking backpackers on a shoestring budget. They thrive on finding the cheapest accommodations, local street food, and off-the-beaten-path attractions. Their resourcefulness leads to authentic travel experiences on a dime

Findings - The Challenges

The process

By narrowing down the problems our travelers might be experiencing we make space for potential features that could be helpful for them in an app

Findings - The Opportunities

The process

ChatGPT gave us suggestions based on the challenges we chose to investigate further and after democratically voting, we chose those 3 features as our focus points

THE APP

Our Aim

In order to create a landing page, we need to know what product we are advertising, so, we finalized the features and later - created mockups for them:

features

• Budget-tracking space

• Accommodation finder

• Local eateries finder

THE WEBSITE

Our Aim

Based on our app we prompted ChatGPT to structure a list of requirements we will include in the landing page, promoting the app. We voted and chose the following:

• How it works

• Key features

• Savings showcase

• Testimonials

• Download prompt

Brand identity

Our Aim

In this step we gave ourselves the space to explore the most, trying out different AI tools for the logo, colors, typography and Tone of voice

The tools:

• ChatGPT

• Adobe Firefly

• Loooka

• LogoAI

• Canva design AI presentation

• Mega creator

The Colors

For the colors we used ChatGPT’s recommendations for effective color palette and prompted Adobe Firefly with the result, we then extracted the suggested pallete from the 1st image --- >

“...should reflect the themes of adventure, resourcefulness, and budget-conscious travel. Here's a color palette suggestion that aligns with these themes: Earthy Greens, Rustic Browns, Ocean Blue, Neutral Tones, Sustainable Teal” - ChatGPT

The Logo

For the logo we used Looka and LogoAI to get us inspired, following the recommendations of ChatGPT for effective logo design. Simplicity and the use of associations helped us choose

“...consider incorporating elements like a backpack, a compass, and maybe a budget-related symbol like a coin. Keep it simple, memorable, and reflective of adventure and affordability.” - ChatGPT

Style tile

The style tile was created to serve us as a guide to ensure consistent design throughout the whole website

We also used ChatGPT for recommendations about the Tone of voice and Typography and simplified the results in the style tile

The illustrations you see were created using Mega Creator’s AI features and Adobe Illustrator.
